Welcome to FSJ

This is the only time you'll ever read about
Fort St. John in your entire life!

Do you ENJOY heavy snowfalls?

Well, you've come to the right place. It snows in September, and doesn't fully disappear until April. We get over 900mm of snow per year, and close to 100 days of snowfall!

Do you FEAR the murderous Moose?

You should. Travel advisory: he is exceedingly grumpy until he's eaten his daily portion of Curious Tourists!... but geez, is he ever majestic

Do you TOLERATE chain restaurants?

What a relief! Because that's all we have! If you enjoy overpriced (yet delicious) nachos, FSJ may just be the place for you.

Do you WORK in the Oil industry?

You'll learn to! You don't have a choice, that's all there is to do! Don't worry, ethical questions aside, it pays very well.

Do you LISTEN to country music?

We don't actually have much live music, but you will hear country everywhere, for the rest of your life, all day, always. Kinda grows on ya after a while! I genuinely love Zach Bryan now and I'm not ashamed.

You will LOVE the Northern Lights.

Joking aside, there are some stunningly beautiful sights in Northern Canada. The people are kind and it is well worth a trip! Head a little farther north and check out the Yukon while you're there. ... just, maybe go in the summer time?
